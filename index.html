About

We are a boutique residential technology consultancy serving homeowners across the South Bay and Peninsula regions of the San Francisco Bay Area.

Founded by a Masters educated computer engineer, our practice combines rigorous technical expertise with a deep commitment to livability. We ensure that technology enhances the home experience rather than complicating it.

We strongly believe in design with purpose and partner closely with homeowners, interior designers, architects, and contractors to deliver integrated technology solutions that are elegant, reliable, and future proof.

Our Design Principles

Integrity

We recommend only what is necessary and appropriate for each home. No upselling, no unnecessary complexity. Just honest, expert guidance.

Precision

Every installation is executed to the highest standard. Clean cable management, proper configuration, and thorough testing are non negotiable.

Simplicity

Technology should disappear into the home. We design systems that are powerful behind the scenes and effortless to use in daily life.

Longevity

We build for the future. We select platforms and hardware that will remain relevant, supported, and expandable for years to come.
